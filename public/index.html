FAQ

What MetaStrip removes and what it does not.

Does MetaStrip keep my files?

No permanent storage is used. Uploaded files are deleted after processing. Cleaned files expire after the configured time or after their first download when that option is enabled.

Will image quality change?

Images are decoded, auto-rotated, and exported at high quality. Video and audio streams are copied without re-encoding. The optional ICC colour profile is preserved for images by default.

Does it remove visible personal information?

No. MetaStrip removes embedded metadata. It does not blur faces, remove visible text, erase watermarks, detect steganography, or remove personal details shown inside the content.

Why does Windows still show a created date?

Windows adds file-system dates when a file is downloaded or copied. Those dates belong to the local file entry and are separate from embedded EXIF or document metadata.

MetaStrip removes standard optional metadata that its supported parsers can detect. No cleaner guarantees removal of unknown proprietary blocks, hidden steganography, or personal details contained in visible or audible content.
